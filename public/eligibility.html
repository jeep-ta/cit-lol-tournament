Eligibility Criteria
Player Requirements
✓
Enrolled CIT StudentMust be currently enrolled in the College of Information Technology at UNO-R for AY 2026–2027.
✓
Valid School IDMust present a valid school ID or enrollment form upon check-in. No ID, no play.
✓
Summoner AccountMust have an active Philippine server (PH) League of Legends account. Smurf accounts are not allowed.
✓
Not Under Academic SuspensionPlayers currently under academic or disciplinary suspension are ineligible.
✓
One Team OnlyA student may only register for one team. Playing for multiple teams will result in disqualification of both teams.
Team Requirements
✓
5 Starters + 1 SubstituteEach team must register exactly 5 main players and 1 substitute (optional). All must meet individual eligibility.
✓
Unique Team NameTeam name must be unique, school-appropriate, and free from offensive content. The committee reserves the right to reject names.
✓
Registration DeadlineTeams must complete registration before the deadline. Late registrations will not be accepted regardless of circumstances.
Prohibited Conduct
✕
No Account Sharing / BoostingUsing someone else's account or playing on a boosted account will result in immediate disqualification and a ban from future CIT tournaments.
✕
No GhostingParticipants who fail to show up for their scheduled match without prior notice forfeit the match. Three forfeits result in team expulsion.
✕
No Cheating / HacksUse of third-party software, scripts, or exploits is strictly prohibited and will result in permanent disqualification.
✕
No Unsportsmanlike BehaviorHarassment, verbal abuse, or threats directed at opponents, spectators, or organizers will result in forfeiture and possible ban.
Match Rules
✓
Remote Play via SpectatorAll matches are played remotely. A designated spectator client will be used to observe and broadcast games.
✓
10-Minute Grace PeriodTeams have 10 minutes to be ready after match call. Failure to comply results in a walkover loss.
✓
Organizer Decision is FinalAll rulings made by the CITSC Tournament Committee are final and not subject to appeal unless a formal protest is filed within 15 minutes.
